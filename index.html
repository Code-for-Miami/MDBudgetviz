Miami-Dade Budget Visualization

Miami's Early Voting for the August 26th Primary Elections starts on August 11th and continues until August 24th.

On Wednesday, August 13, Miami-Dade Mayor Carlos Gimenez will host two "Social Media Town Hall Meetings." Each will offer a chance for citizens to ask the mayor any question regarding the proposed budget for the 2014-2015 fiscal year. From 12pm - 1pm, Gimenez will be on Facebook. From 3pm - 4pm, he will be on Twitter.

We aim to use visualizations to make the proposal as easy to understand as possible for the greater Miami-Dade population. We take our numbers from the Miami-Dade Budget website. The data used is accurate up to August 11, 2014.

All citizens deserve to know where their tax dollars go.
